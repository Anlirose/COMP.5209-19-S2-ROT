About Us

In the begining Melting Pot Cafe' was started on March 2016 mainly focussing on the staffs and students of Toi Ohomai Institution.
If serving authentic Kiwi food at affordable prices in a comfortable and friendly atmosphere that caters equally well for Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner, Melting Pot Cafe ' is well known for producing magnificent Cakes & Goodies so that you can enjoy a delicious treat or relax with a coffee.
